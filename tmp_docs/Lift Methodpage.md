###### 6. BENEFITS STACK

When you implement the LIFT Method, you completely change the math of your wealth:

* **The 0% Floor:** Your cash value is linked to a market index, but not *in* the market. If the market drops 20%, your return is simply zero.  **You never lose a dime of your principal to a market crash** .
* **No Miracles Needed:** Because you never lose ground during bad years, you don't need a miracle to recover. You just need one good year of gains to experience dramatic compounding.
* **Double-Duty Dollars:** Instead of withdrawing money and interrupting your growth, you *borrow* against your policy. Your full cash value continues to earn indexed returns, while you deploy that borrowed capital into real estate or other income-producing assets. Your money works in two places at once.
* **Tax-Advantaged Cash Flow:** By utilizing policy loans, you can access your capital and generate spendable income without triggering massive tax bombs.
